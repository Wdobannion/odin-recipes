Blue Cheese Burger

Description

A blue cheese burger is truly a marvelous dish for any lover of beef and burgers.

A burger by itself is good but the blue cheese really compliments what is already a masterpiece of the culinary art.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Minced fresh chives
  • Blue cheese crumbles
  • Salt
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • Dry mustard
  • Hot pepper sauce
  • Hamburger buns

Directions

  1. Mix ground beef,chives,blue cheese,salt,Worcestershire sauce,pepper,mustard,and hot pepper sauce together in a large bowl until well combined.
  2. When ready to cook,preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Remove beef mixture from the refrigerator and form the patties
  4. Cook patties on the preheated grill until at least 160 degrees F(71 degrees C) about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve on hamburger buns.
